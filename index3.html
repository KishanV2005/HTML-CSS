Story Overview

  • Attack on Titan is a dark fantasy anime series set in a world where humanity is under attack by giant humanoid creatures known as Titans.
  • The story follows Eren Yeager and his friends Mikasa and Armin as they join the military to fight against the Titans and uncover the secrets behind their existence.

Main Characters

  1. Eren Yeager: The main protagonist who possesses the ability to transform into the Attack Titan.
  2. Mikasa Ackerman: Eren's childhood friend and a skilled fighter.
  3. Armin Arlert: Eren's friend and a strategic thinker.

Series Details

  1. Manga: Written and illustrated by Hajime Isayama, the manga series was published from 2009 to 2021 with 34 volumes.
  2. Anime: Produced by Wit Studio and MAPPA, the anime series consists of four seasons, with the final season concluding in 2023.

    3. Premiere Dates

    1. Season 1: April 7, 2013
    2. Season 2: April 1, 2017
    3. Season 3: July 23, 2018
    4. Season 4: December 7, 2020

    Awards and Reception

    Attack on Titan has received widespread critical acclaim and has won several awards, including the Kodansha Manga Award, the Attilio Micheluzzi Award, and the Harvey Award. The series has sold over 140 million copies worldwide, making it one of the best-selling manga series of all time.

    Themes

    1. Survival: The struggle for humanity's survival against the Titans.
    2. Friendship: The bonds between Eren, Mikasa, and Armin.
    3. Trauma: The psychological effects of trauma on the charactes.
    4. Power: The struggle for power and control in a world under threat.

    Impact

    Attack on Titan has had a significant impact on popular culture, inspiring numerous adaptations, including live-action films, video games, and merchandise. The series has also sparked a dedicated fan base, with fans worldwide creating fan art, cosplay, and fan fiction.
